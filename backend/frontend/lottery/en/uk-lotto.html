[UK] UK Lotto

UK Lotto is one of the most popular lottery games in UK, using a 6/59 number format. Each ticket costs £2, with draws held on Wed/Sat at 20:00 GMT. Players select 6 numbers from 1 to 59.

The jackpot odds are 1:45,057,474, but with a rollover mechanism, unclaimed jackpots accumulate into the next draw, often reaching staggering amounts. The overall odds of winning any prize are approximately 1:9.3.
