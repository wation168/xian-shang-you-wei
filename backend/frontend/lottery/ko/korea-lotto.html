[KR] 로또 6/45

로또 6/45 is one of the most popular lottery games in Korea, using a 6/45+1/45 number format. Each ticket costs ₩1,000, with draws held on Sat at 20:45 KST. Players select 6 numbers from 1 to 45and 1 bonus number(s) from 1 to 45.

The jackpot odds are 1:8,145,060, but with a rollover mechanism, unclaimed jackpots accumulate into the next draw, often reaching staggering amounts. The overall odds of winning any prize are approximately 1:45.
